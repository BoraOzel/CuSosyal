Privacy Policy — Last updated: June 2026

Overview

ÇÜ Sosyal is an iOS application that helps university students discover campus clubs and events. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

Data We Collect

  • Email address — Required for account creation and authentication.
  • Full name — Provided by the user to be displayed on their profile page.
  • Profile photo — Optional. Stored only if the user chooses to upload one.
  • Community logos — Images uploaded exclusively by community administrators.
  • Interest tags — Selected by the user to personalize the AI chat assistant.

How We Use Your Data

Collected data is used solely for the core functionality of the app:

  • Creating and managing user accounts
  • Event reservations and tracking
  • Personalizing the AI assistant
  • Displaying community profile pages

Your data is never shared with third parties and is never used for advertising purposes.

Data Storage

All user data is stored on Google's Firebase infrastructure (Firestore and Cloud Storage). Firebase complies with industry-standard security protocols.

The Gemini AI chat feature operates via Google's Gemini API. Chat history is not permanently stored on the device or on our servers.

Device Permissions

  • Calendar — Used to add events to your device calendar. Calendar data is never sent to our servers.
  • Photo library — Used only by community administrators to upload a community logo.

Account Deletion

To permanently delete your account and all associated data:

In-app: Profile tab → Delete Account → Confirm your password.

This action permanently deletes your user profile from Firestore and your Firebase Authentication record. It cannot be undone.

Children's Privacy

ÇÜ Sosyal is intended for university students and does not target users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.
